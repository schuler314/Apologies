Player Guide

Objective

Be the first player to move all four pawns from your Start space to your Home space. Pawns move clockwise based on the drawn card.

Leaving Start

Draw a 1 or 2 to move a pawn out of Start. Drawing a 2 grants an extra turn!

Special Action Cards

  • 6 EARTHQUAKE: Move 6. Opponents on board are knocked back 1 space!
  • 9 BULLDOZER: Move 9. Bump ANY opponent pawns you pass through!
  • 13 APOLOGIES!: Swap a pawn from Start with an opponent's, bumping them.
  • 14 SAFETY DASH: Move from Start straight to your Safety Zone entrance.

Bumps & Slides

Land on an opponent's pawn to bump them back to Start.

Land on the start of a slide (not your color) to automatically slide to the end, violently bumping ANY pawns in your path!

Powerups & Hazards

  • Springboards: Launch forward +4 spaces.
  • Portals: Warp instantly to the connected portal!
  • Mud Pits: Get stuck and dragged back -4 spaces.
  • Trapdoors: Fall completely back to Start!
